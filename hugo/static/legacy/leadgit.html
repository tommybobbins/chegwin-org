Leaving Amanda

MARTIN MANNING

Age : 22

Born :Grimsby

Martin, the rhythm guitarist, was born and brought up in and around Grimsby.

He says his childhood was nothing special, he was just a kid in a mans world. He needed something more, even from childhood he had a vision. His main aim in life was to have fun, he didn't care how he got it or who he trampled on to get it.

Martin said " When I was young I used to hassle my mum constantly and when she wasn't giving in I'd just start crying, it was a good way of winning. I kept that up until I was about fourteen and then I found out it wasn't cool to cry. I used to get into loads of fights about it at school and I suppose I was a bit of a loner."

It hasn't seemed to affect Martin now though, well not on the surface anyway, he seems normal enough.
