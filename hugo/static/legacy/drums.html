Leaving Amanda

MAYNEY

Age: 21

Born: South Sheilds, Tyne and Wear.

She bangs the Drums

With her super-model good looks, she catches the eye of all the young men who turn up to Leaving Amanda's live shows. The slow tunes in the bands discography must have all been written based on this one person.

She drums with a passion like no other, even Phil Collins and Mick Fleetwood haven't got close to doing things with drums that come so naturally to our little South Shields sex bomb. Known as `panther' within the band due to her sleek looks and hardwoman of the band attitude, she's one cat that all the boys would like to get their teeth into.
